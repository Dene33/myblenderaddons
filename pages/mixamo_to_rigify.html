What is the Mixamo to Rigify?

Mixamo to Rigify is the add-on for the simple and hassle-free import of Mixamo animations to Blender.

Key Features

  • No messing with Blender's metarig, it's created automatically (you can tweak it later and regenerate Rigify if you want to)
  • One-click - just select mixamo .fbx and click "import"
  • Option to import multiple Rigify's at once (as individually created Rigify rigs or just one Rigify with multiple animations on it)
  • Option to import multiple Rigify animations to single Rigify that you already have in the Blender scene
