What is the Center of Mass Add-on?

The Blender Center of Mass Add-on is a powerful tool designed to enhance your animation workflow by accurately calculating the center of mass for any character or object. Perfect for animators and game developers, this add-on ensures your characters stay balanced and grounded, bringing a new level of realism to your projects.

Key Features

  • Automatic Center of Mass Calculation: Instantly calculate the center of mass for any rigged character or object and project it onto the floor plane
  • Dynamic Mass Distribution: Easily assign and adjust masses to bones or objects based on realistic human body mass distributions, allowing for dynamic tweaking
  • Multiple Centers of Mass: Create and manage multiple centers of mass for different objects or characters within a single scene
  • Balance Visualization: Visualize the area between points of contact (e.g., feet) to see if the character is balanced or likely to fall
  • Customizable Settings: Tweak masses and visualization parameters to fit your specific needs, ensuring perfect balance in your animations
