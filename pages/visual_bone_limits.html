What is the Visual Bone Limits?

Easily constrain the bone rotation to mesh surfaces with just one click. Say goodbye to the buggy Limit Rotation constraint and see a clear visual representation of the rotation limit space. Rigify compatible.

Key Features

  • Easily constrain bone rotation to mesh surfaces with a single click.
  • Select the desired limit type from a drop-down menu.
  • Compatible with various Rigify bone types such as spines.super_head, limbs.super_limb, limbs.arm, limbs.leg, and basic.super_copy.
  • Custom meshes as limit types
